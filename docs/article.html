Blackbeard's facial hair actually red

Blackbeard allegedly couldn't think of another name for himself since Redbeard was already taken.

The pirate did not think Crimsonbeard, Maroonbeard, and Rubybeard were catchy enough.

Blackbeard even considered Bloodbeard, but he thought it would be too obvious as a pirate: there could be other reasons for blood to be in someone's beard, such as dismembering, slicing, destroying, etc.
